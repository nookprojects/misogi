About the App

Misogi Journal is a daily journaling and goal-tracking app inspired by the Japanese purification ritual and the modern challenge framework popularised by Jesse Itzler — choosing one bold, uncomfortable goal each year that forces real growth.

Most goal-setting tools ask you to juggle a dozen priorities. Misogi Journal asks for one. One impossible thing. Then shows up with you for 365 days.

Features

  • Define and commit to your annual Misogi challenge
  • Daily journal prompts tailored to your goal
  • Mood and momentum tracking across the year
  • Monthly review and milestone check-ins
  • Mental toughness prompts for the hard days
  • Year-end reflection and transformation summary
  • Streak tracking to build the daily habit

Support

Having trouble, or just want to say hello? We're here.

How do I choose my Misogi?

Your Misogi should genuinely scare you — roughly a 50% chance of success this year. Physical, creative, professional, or personal. The app guides you through a setup flow to help you define and commit.

Can I change my Misogi mid-year?

Yes. We encourage reflection first — wanting to quit is often part of the process. The app lets you amend your goal with a journal entry, keeping your growth record honest.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. Your Apple ID will verify any prior subscriptions.

How do I cancel Misogi Plus?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Misogi Journal → Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my data?

Local data is removed when you delete the app. For any account or Supabase data, email privacy@misogijournal.com and we'll action it within 7 days.
