About the App

Misogi Journal is a daily journaling app inspired by the Japanese ritual of purification and the modern challenge framework popularised by Jesse Itzler — choosing one bold, uncomfortable goal each year that forces real growth.

Most goal-setting tools ask you to juggle a dozen priorities. Misogi Journal asks you to choose one thing. One impossible thing. Then show up for 365 days.

Features

  • Set and commit to your annual Misogi goal
  • Daily reflection prompts tailored to your challenge
  • Progress tracking across the full year
  • Journal entries with mood and momentum markers
  • Monthly review and milestone check-ins
  • Mental toughness prompts for hard days
  • Year-end reflection and transformation summary

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose my Misogi?

Your Misogi should be something that genuinely scares you — something you have roughly a 50% chance of achieving this year. It can be physical, creative, professional, or personal. The app walks you through a guided setup to help you define and commit to your challenge.

Can I change my Misogi mid-year?

Yes, though we encourage deep reflection first. A Misogi is designed to be uncomfortable — wanting to change it is often part of the process. The app lets you amend your goal with a journal entry explaining the change, so your growth journey remains honest and intact.

Is my journal data backed up?

Your journal data is stored locally on your device. We recommend regularly backing up your device via iCloud or iTunes to ensure your entries are not lost.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your App Store account will be used to verify any prior purchases.

How do I delete my account or data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete the app to remove all data. To delete specific journal entries, use the delete option within each entry inside the app.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how Misogi Journal ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Misogi Journal is designed with privacy in mind. The app stores all journal entries, goals, and personal reflections locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal journal data on any external servers.

We may collect the following limited, non-personal data:

  • Anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs (via Apple's built-in crash reporting)
  • Anonymous usage analytics to understand general app usage patterns (e.g. feature usage, session length)

This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not read, access, or transmit your journal entries or goal data
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising purposes
  • We do not use tracking technologies or third-party advertising SDKs

In-App Purchases

Any purchases made within the app are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Misogi Journal is intended for users aged 17 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. Deleting the app removes all associated data. There is no account to delete on our end.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple frameworks (such as StoreKit for purchases and CloudKit for optional iCloud backup). These are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
